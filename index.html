Featured Case Studies

Proven Business Impact

Customer 360 Analytics Platform

Business Challenge: Customer data was scattered across multiple disconnected systems with no single source of truth — resulting in inconsistent reporting, duplicate records, and CRM teams working from incomplete or conflicting customer information. There was no way to build a unified view of the customer without manually reconciling data from multiple sources.

Impact: Designed and delivered a Customer 360 data warehouse that consolidated fragmented customer data from 5+ source systems into a single trusted foundation — eliminating cross-report discrepancies, resolving duplicate records, and integrating the warehouse directly with the internal CRM so customer-facing teams always operate on clean, complete, and consistent data.

CRM Analytics Data Quality Stakeholder Collaboration

Enterprise Integration Platform - API Gateway Layer

Business Challenge: AMAN's operations depended on disconnected systems — CRM, billing aggregators, external AI models, and internal databases operated in silos, requiring manual data exchange and custom point-to-point connections for every new integration. Each new partner or service added disproportionate effort and risk.

Impact: Standardized data exchange across internal systems and external partners via secure API gateway — improving business process automation, reducing integration effort for new services, and enabling reliable real-time data availability.

API Integration System Architecture Process Automation Business Process Integration

DevOps Analytics & Performance Reporting

Business Challenge: Engineering leadership lacked visibility into software delivery performance, project progress, and pipeline health — decisions about DevOps effectiveness and team performance were based on anecdotal reporting with no standardized metrics.

Impact: Delivered two complementary reporting streams that gave leadership a complete picture of engineering performance — extracted and visualized Jira project data in Power BI to support project management reporting and progress tracking; connected Jenkins pipeline logs to Kibana to enable real-time pipeline status monitoring and applied DORA metrics framework to measure delivery performance objectively. Enabled evidence-based quarterly performance reviews and DevOps investment decisions for the first time.

Power BI KPI Design Executive Reporting ELK

VeriCash Enterprise Data Warehouse

Business Challenge: Business reporting relied on fragmented operational systems with no centralized analytical platform — making reporting slow, inconsistent, and impossible to scale. The organization needed a validated, requirements-driven approach before committing to a full warehouse implementation.

Impact: Contributed as part of the project team — supporting requirements gathering with business stakeholders, evaluating and documenting tool options through structured POCs, mapping data specifications and business definitions, defining and applying ETL jobs to move data into the analytical layer, and building prototype dashboards to validate analytical use cases before full implementation.
